# Monetization Plan: The GAMESA Economy

## 1. Business Model Overview
We adopt an **Open Core / Dual Licensing** model.
- **Core Framework (GAMESA)**: Open Source (MIT). Free for personal, research, and non-commercial leverage.
- **Enterprise Nexus (KrystalStack Pro)**: Commercial License. Includes advanced orchestration, certified drivers, and SLA support.

## 2. Revenue Streams

### A. Corporate Sponsorship & Foundation Membership
*For companies who want to steer the future of the Industrial Organism.*
- **Platinum Member ($50k/yr)**: Board seat on the Foundation, priority feature roadmap, logo on main README.
- **Gold Member ($20k/yr)**: Voting rights on diverse technical commitees, quarterly strategy calls.
- **Silver Member ($5k/yr)**: Logo on website, access to beta builds.

### B. Enterprise Licensing (Self-Hosted)
*For Data Centers and High-Frequency Trading firms.*
- **Per-Node Licensing**: Annual subscription per GPU/Node managed by KrystalStack.
- **Includes**:
    - Validated "Safety Contracts" (Insurance-grade reliability).
    - Long Term Support (LTS) releases.
    - "Shadow Council" Audit modules (Compliance logging).

### C. Consulting & Integration Services
*custom implementation of the "Industrial Organism".*
- **Architecture Validation**: Reviewing client infrastructure for "Organism Readiness."
- **Custom Adapters**: Writing specialized "Spinal Reflex" drivers for proprietary hardware (e.g., legacy CNC machines, custom ASICs).

### D. "The Marketplace" (Future)
- A specialized App Store for "Optimization Genes."
- Community members list optimized logic blocks; we take a % transaction fee.
